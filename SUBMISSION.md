## Short Description

Investors Helper is an interactive Shiny dashboard for studying the long-term trade-off between stocks and bonds. The Portfolio page compares two strategies under a shared time horizon and risk-aversion setting. The Goal Planner evaluates allocations from 0% to 100% stocks, estimates their probability of reaching a real purchasing-power target, and lets the user select a table row to inspect the linked outcome distribution.

The model uses equity, inflation, and Treasury-yield parameters derived from `VFINX`, FRED `CPIAUCSL`, and FRED `GS10`. It evaluates 999 deterministic percentile scenarios per allocation. It is an educational scenario model rather than a historical backtest or financial-advice tool.

## Dashboard Tasks

1. Compare two stock/bond strategies.
2. Explore expected wealth and uncertainty over time.
3. Analyze the effect of risk aversion on utility.
4. Compare allocations by goal probability and downside risk.
5. Select an allocation and inspect its detailed outcome distribution.
